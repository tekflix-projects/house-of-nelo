Good to Know

Questions,
answered.

Do you work outside Abuja?

Yes. CSI Designs is based in Abuja and regularly designs across the USA and internationally. Travel is built into the proposal, and e-design makes remote collaboration seamless.

How long does a project take?

A room refresh can take a few weeks; a full home typically runs three to nine months depending on scope, sourcing, and construction. We'll map a realistic timeline in your proposal.

Can you work with my architect or builder?

Absolutely — collaboration is one of our strengths. Chinelo has spent years partnering with architects and builders to bring cohesive visions to life.

What's the first step?

Share your project through our inquiry form. We'll review the details and schedule a discovery call to see if we're the right fit.
