Meet the Designer

Chinelo.

Founder & Principal Designer of House of Nelo — creating memories and magic in people's homes for over five years.

Based in Abuja, Nigeria · Designing across the USA & worldwide

A designer who begins with the story.

I've always believed a home is more than four walls and beautiful things — it's the backdrop to a life. My work is about listening deeply, then curating spaces that hold the way you live, love, and gather.

I earned my BFA in Interior Design from the Savannah College of Art & Design (SCAD), one of the world's leading design schools. Since then, I've spent more than five years turning clients' visions into reality — working shoulder-to-shoulder with architects, builders, and artisans to bring cohesive, soulful spaces to life.

My aesthetic is warm and collected: natural materials, honest textures, layered light, and pieces with a story. I draw on my roots and my travels between Nigeria and the United States to create interiors that feel both globally sophisticated and deeply personal. Whether I'm styling a single room or shaping a home from the studs up, my goal is the same — to design a space that makes you exhale the moment you walk in.
