The Philosophy

We design for the way you live.

Every project begins with a conversation — about your rituals, your memories, the life you want your space to hold. Then we translate that into rooms that feel effortless, layered, and unmistakably yours.

Trained at the Savannah College of Art & Design and shaped by five years alongside architects and master builders, Chinelo blends the discipline of great architecture with the warmth of a well-loved home. The result is interiors that feel collected over a lifetime — not decorated in a weekend.

The Process

Calm, considered,
and clear from
day one.

Great design shouldn't feel stressful. Our four-phase process keeps you informed and inspired at every step.

01

Discovery

We talk through your vision, your lifestyle, your budget, and the feeling you want to walk into every day. This is where the magic begins.

02

Concept & Design

Mood, materials, layouts, and 3D visuals — a complete design story so you can see and feel the space before a single item is ordered.

03

Curation & Build

We source, procure, and manage the trades — coordinating every delivery and detail so you don't have to lift a finger.

04

The Reveal

We style every surface and hand you the keys to a home that feels like it was always meant to be yours.
